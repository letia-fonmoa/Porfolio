All Things Music

Music Career:

Rihanna moved to the United States at the age of 16, to start off her music career after being discovered by Evan Rogers in Barbados. At the age of 18 she was signed up to Def Jam Records who had a new president, rapper Jay-Z. From there, she released her first song “Pon de Replay” in 2005 reaching number 2 on the Billboard singles chart. Soon afterwards she released her album, “A Girl Like Me”, with one of her songs, SOS, reaching number 1 on Billboard and being her first number one hit. Her success in the music industry only went up, achieving 14 number one hits, 31 in the top 10 and 62 in the top 100 Billboard charts (a combination of collaborations and her own singles). Rihanna has also collaborated with artists such as Eminem, Beyonce, Jay-Z, Drake and many more.

In 2015, Rihanna partnered with Samsung to give an experience both “mobile...and intimate real world (Vega, 2016) before the release of her album “ANTI”. Together, “ANTIdiaRy” was created releasing episodes of Rihanna’s journey from a young girl up until her success in the music industry. Each week, new content was released giving clues to Samsung users to uncover and experience ANTIdiaRY in the real world. Then on January 27th, 2016, the ANTI album was released but only accessed through Samsung phones. It was streamed more than 13 million times and had 1.4 million downloads in the matter of 14 hours, making this the fastest album to go platinum ever.

Currently, she has not released any new music except her collaboration with PARTYNEXTDOOR “Believe It” , and is focusing more on her cosmetic and clothing line.

Awards

Overall, Rihanna has won 181 awards and had 633 nominations from all over the world. This includes 13 wins and 26 nominations from the AMA’s (American Music Awards), 12 wins and 66 nominations from the BMA’s (Billboard Music Awards) and 9 wins and 33 nominations from the Grammy Awards. She was also nominated for “Best Commercial and Best Partnership” in the Clio Awards for the production of the “ANTIdiaRy” online and offline experience, with Samsung.
