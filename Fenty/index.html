All Things Rihanna

The Barbados born R&B artist rose to stardom after being discovered by American record producer, Evan Rogers and soon secured a contract with Def Jam record label. As successful as she is in the music industry, the now 32-year old has become one of the most influential people expanding beyond the music industry tapping into the fashion industry and starting her own make up and clothing brand, as well as starting her own foundation to help those in poverty.
